Our Team

Since his call to the Nigerian Bar in 1989 and years of private and corporate legal practice, Mr. Albert has consulted for organizations and institutions in different sectors of the Nigerian economy. After a brief stint as an Associate Legal Practitioner for 3 years, Mr. Albert became the Company Secretary of a fast-growing Savings and Loan Institution in 1993.

From there he joined the Coscharis Group in 1994 and rose to the position of General Counsel in 2000. He quit paid employment to become an entrepreneur in 2003, after co-founding Eagle Eye Communications, advertising, and branding agency responsible for several brand positioning at that time. He eventually co-founded Legalpedia in 2005. Legalpedia is the most comprehensive electronic library of legal resources in Nigeria. He is also a Senior Partner in Keystone Attorneys, an ICT-driven law firm based in Lagos.

Mr. Albert is currently the Editor-in-Chief of Legalpedia and also a Consultant to the United Nations on Justice Sector Reforms. He has trained several lawyers, law teachers, law students, and judges on the use of ICT in the legal profession. He led a team of Judicial officers to Singapore in 2012 on an UN-coordinated study tour sponsored by the Royal Kingdom of the Netherlands.

Mr. Albert has undergone technological training in the USA, Dubai, Singapore, and leadership training at the Lagos Business School. He holds a Master’s degree in Law and is a Director in several companies, including servicing companies in the Oil and Gas sector, where he consults as an expert in Local Content.
