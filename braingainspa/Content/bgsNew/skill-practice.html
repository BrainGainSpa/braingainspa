Skill Practice

It features a comprehensive British/American Curriculum as well as Nigerian/Ghanaian Curriculum. It provides thousands of quizzes ranging from Grades 1-12 and covering topics from the various under listed subjects (Maths, English, Basic Science & Technology, Civic Education, Business Studies, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Further Maths, Agricultural Science, Economics, Accounting, Commerce, Literature, Government, History, Fine & Applied Arts…). The Skill Practice resource is designed to help reinforce topics already taught in the Classroom.

A User is timed, scored and graded for every Exercise, and invariably use this resource to Self-Learn. There is a monthly optional Mock Exam attached to this Resource for those interested in getting rewarded for Outstanding Performances and also have their pictures displayed on the BrainGain Faces of the month.

In addition, Certificates of Achievements are presented to regular Users who have achieved the following Milestones of correctly answered questions (500, 1000,2000,3000,4500,6000). Furthermore, 4500 Milestone achievers gets selected and become eligible for a chance to win a Scholarship. This Skill Practice resource is truly designed to be exciting and rewarding for Users as they can also Earn while Learning.
